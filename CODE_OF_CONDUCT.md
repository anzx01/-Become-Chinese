# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior and the consequences of violations.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of different perspectives and experiences
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being constructive in feedback and criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Offensive language or personal attacks
- Unwelcome advances or attention
- Trolling, spamming, or disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Violations of others' intellectual property

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for maintaining a respectful environment. They will:

- Clarify standards of acceptable behavior
- Address reported violations promptly and fairly
- Remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code
- Maintain confidentiality of reporters

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, commits) and community interactions related to this project.

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness violations:

1. Contact the project maintainers via private email
2. Do not engage in public disputes
3. Provide context and examples when possible
4. Allow maintainers time to respond

## Consequences

Violations may result in:

- Warnings or temporary restrictions on participation
- Removal from discussions or projects
- Permanent bans from the community (for severe cases)
